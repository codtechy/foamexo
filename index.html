Asked Questions

Your comfort is our priority. Here are answers to the most common questions about Foamexo mattresses.

What makes Foamexo mattresses different from others?

Foamexo mattresses combine cutting-edge technology with premium materials to provide the perfect balance of comfort and support. Our mattresses are designed to suit different sleep preferences, helping you achieve restful, uninterrupted sleep every night.

How long will my Foamexo mattress last?

With proper care, a Foamexo mattress can last up to 10 years or more. We use high-quality, durable materials that resist sagging and wear, ensuring your mattress stays comfortable and supportive for years.

Can I try the mattress before buying?

Yes, we offer a trial period for you to experience the mattress in the comfort of your own home. If you're not satisfied, you can return it for a full refund.

How do I care for my Foamexo mattress?

Foamexo mattresses are low-maintenance. Simply rotate it every 3-6 months to ensure even wear. Use a mattress protector to keep it clean, and avoid jumping or putting heavy weights on the bed to maintain its shape.
